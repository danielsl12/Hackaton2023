Eligibility conditions are affected by:

medical condition
Who is eligible?

Everyone.
What is the eligibility?

A series of 5 allergy shots.

Additional vaccinations according to the decision of an allergy doctor (allergist).
How much does it cost?
NIS 185 for a series of 5 vaccines
NIS 37 for each additional vaccine

     The final cost will be calculated according to the number of series that will be required to complete the medical treatment.
     Payment is made at the allergist's clinic.

Where is the service provided?

In the allergology clinics of Maccabi.
to locate an allergist
what should you do?

     A referral must be obtained from the attending physician.

What else should you know?

     The allergist prepares the vaccines in his clinic, depending on the allergy you suffer from.
     You may need more than one series of vaccines, at the allergist's discretion.
     The cost of each additional vaccine - NIS 37.
     Payment is made directly at the allergist's clinic.
