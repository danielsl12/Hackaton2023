Types of insurance plans
Who's eligible?

My friends Maccabi, Maccabi Gold and Maccabi Silver.
What's the entitlement?

Testing to diagnose allergic reactions to a variety of substances, according to indicated by referring to an allergologist.

 
Learn more about allergy testing
How much does it cost?

Deductible of NIS 221 per referral (regardless of the amount of tests required).
Where is the service provided?
Maccabi Laboratories
What should you do?

 Get a referral from an allergologist.

Summoning a doctor's appointment

 Make an appointment to perform the examination in the laboratory or medical center of Maccabi.

What else is worth knowing?

Eligibility for testing is a unique entitlement to members of Maccabi Shelly, Maccabi Zahab and Maccabi Silver. Anyone who is not a member of one of these insurance plans will not be able to take the test, either at full cost.

Payment is by referral, regardless of the amount of allergens that appear in the referral or the number of tests.

A new invocation would require a rearranged deductible payment.

Avoid taking antihistamines (any antiallergic drug) for 7 days before the test. Taking antihistamines does not allow making you notice skin for clarification of allergy.
